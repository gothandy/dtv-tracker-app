Privacy Policy for DTV Tracker

Dean Trail Volunteers — Registered Charity 1208988
Last updated: March 2026

This privacy policy explains how the DTV Tracker application (tracker.dtv.org.uk) collects, uses, and stores your personal data. It applies specifically to this application. For the full Dean Trail Volunteers privacy notice, see our DTV Privacy Notice (PDF).

Who we are

Dean Trail Volunteers is a registered charity (number 1208988) that manages volunteer activity in the Forest of Dean. DTV Tracker is an internal tool used to record volunteer hours, session attendance, and group membership.

What data we collect

  • Name and email address — used to identify your volunteer profile and match you to sessions.
  • Session attendance and hours — records of sessions you have attended and hours contributed.
  • Consents and records — privacy consent, photo consent, newsletter consent, and charity membership status.
  • Photos and videos — media you upload relating to specific sessions.
  • Authentication details — when you sign in via Google or Microsoft, we receive your name, email address, and a unique account identifier from those providers. We do not store passwords.

How we use your data

  • To record and report volunteer hours and session attendance.
  • To manage your membership and consent records.
  • To enable you to sign up for sessions and upload media.
  • To produce anonymised or aggregated statistics about volunteer activity.

Who can see your data

  • Admins and Check-In staff — can view and edit all volunteer data.
  • Read-only staff — can view all data but cannot make changes.
  • You (self-service login) — can view your own profile, sessions, and uploaded media.
  • Public (unauthenticated) — can view sessions, groups, and aggregate statistics. Individual volunteer names and profiles are not visible without logging in.

Third-party services

  • Google, Facebook, and Microsoft (Entra ID) — used for authentication. Governed by their respective privacy policies.
  • Eventbrite — if you register for a DTV event on Eventbrite, your name and email may be imported into DTV Tracker to create or update your volunteer profile.
  • Microsoft SharePoint — all application data is stored in SharePoint Online on servers within the Microsoft 365 tenancy operated by Dean Trail Volunteers.

How long we keep your data

Volunteer records are retained for as long as you are an active volunteer, and for a reasonable period thereafter in line with our full privacy notice. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact the DTV data controller as described in our full privacy notice.

Cookies and sessions

DTV Tracker uses a session cookie to keep you logged in. This cookie is essential for the application to function and is deleted when you log out or close your browser session. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact Dean Trail Volunteers via the details in our full privacy notice.
